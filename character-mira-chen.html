🔮 The Inventor

Mira Chen

⚙️ Engineer 💡 Innovator 🔬 Scientist

Mira Chen is a genius engineer whose curiosity knows no bounds. From a young age she dismantled clocks and radios just to understand what made them tick — and then made them tick better. Her workshop is a chaotic wonderland of half-finished prototypes and breakthrough ideas.

She joined the team after building a device in her garage that impressed everyone who saw it. Mira approaches every problem like a puzzle waiting to be solved, often working through the night fueled by coffee and sheer determination. "If it doesn't exist yet, I'll build it," she often says.
