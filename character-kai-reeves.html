Kai Reeves

🏋️ Warrior 🛡️ Protector ❤️ Heart

Kai Reeves is fiercely loyal and always ready for action — the team's unbreakable protector. Growing up in a rough neighborhood, Kai learned to fight not out of aggression but out of necessity, defending those who couldn't defend themselves.

Despite a tough exterior, Kai has a warm heart and a dry sense of humor that catches people off guard. In battle, Kai is the last one standing and the first to act. Off the battlefield, you'll find Kai tinkering with vintage motorcycles or cooking elaborate meals for the team. "I don't fight because I want to — I fight because you're behind me."
