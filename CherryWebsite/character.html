📜 Lore

Born under a blood‑moon eclipse, Eldrin was cast out from the elven enclave of Silvanus for his forbidden pact with a sentient twilight blade named Umbrathil. The weapon whispers secrets from the Shadowfell, granting him unnatural charisma at the cost of eternal twilight in his soul. Now he roams the borderlands as a mercenary, searching for a way to break the bond without losing his own identity.

🗡️ Deeds

✦ Defeated the Ashen Knight in single combat under the Ghostlight Bridge.
✦ Uncovered the lost Tome of Eclipses in the Sunken Crypts of Myr.
✦ Forged an uneasy alliance with the Raven Queen’s emissary to seal a demonic rift.
✦ Freed the village of Thornhollow from a curse that turned its people into crystal statues.
