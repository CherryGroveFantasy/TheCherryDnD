🌿 The Healer

Luna Park

🌿 Nature 💚 Empathy ✨ Spirit

Luna Park is gentle yet unbreakable, keeping the team whole — both physically and emotionally. Raised by her grandmother in a mountain village, Luna learned the healing arts from plants, patience, and silence long before she ever held a modern medical kit.

Her empathy is her greatest weapon — she can read a room the way others read maps. Where others see lost causes, Luna sees people who haven't been given the right care yet. She carries the weight of her team's struggles quietly, always with a steady hand and a warm word. "Everyone heals at their own pace. I'll be here when you're ready."
